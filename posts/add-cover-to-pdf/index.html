Add Cover to PDF

2021-05-05

Procedure

First, convert image.jpg to image.pdf through:

convert image.jpg image.pdf

Second, combine image.pdf with input.pdf to produce output.pdf through:

pdfunite image.pdf input.pdf output.pdf
